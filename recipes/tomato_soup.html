Tomato soup

Description

A recipe for a delicious tomato soup

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Vegetable stock
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Herbs

Steps

  1. Cut the onions and bake in a large pot
  2. Cut the garlic and add them to the onions
  3. Add the tomatoes and herbs
  4. Add 1 liter vegetable stock to the pan and simmer for at least one hour
  5. Use a blender to get the desired thickness
