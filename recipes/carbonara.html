Carbonara

Description

A recipe for a delicious carbonara

Ingredients

  • Parmesan cheese
  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Guanciale
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Cut the guanciale in small cubes and bake in a pan
  2. Mix the eggs and add black pepper and parmesan cheese
  3. Cook the spaghetti and save the pasta water afterwards
  4. Combine the spaghetti with the guanciale, egg mixture and add some pasta water
  5. Add some black pepper and parmesan to taste
