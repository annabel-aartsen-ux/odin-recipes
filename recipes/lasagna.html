Lasagna

Description

A recipe for a delicious lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Tomatoes
  • Minced beef
  • Celery
  • Carrot
  • Tomato paste
  • Milk
  • Flower
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Nutmeg
  • Onion
  • Rosemary
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Cut the celery, onion and carrot in small pieces
  2. Bake the cut vegetables on low heat and add tomato paste after 5 minutes
  3. Add the minced beef and bake untill it's completely baked
  4. Cut the tomatoes and add to the meat and vegetables
  5. Add the rosemary and simmer for at least one hour
  6. Add butter to a different pan and melt completely
  7. Add the flower to the butter and let it bake until the "dough" is lightly brown
  8. Add the milk little by little
  9. Add nutmeg and parmesan cheese to taste
  10. Assemble the ingredients in a baking dish
